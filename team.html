Meet Our Team

The talented individuals behind Zylox's success

Leadership

The visionaries guiding our path

Moeed Mirza

Founder & CEO

Moeed founded Zylox in 2019 after working as a freelance developer for several years. With a background in computer science and business administration, he brings a unique blend of technical expertise and entrepreneurial vision to the company.

Under his leadership, Zylox has grown from a one-person operation to a full-service digital agency serving clients globally. Moeed is passionate about leveraging technology to solve business challenges and is hands-on in guiding the strategic direction of client projects.

I believe that great technology should empower businesses, not complicate them. Our mission at Zylox is to make cutting-edge digital solutions accessible to all businesses, helping them connect with their audience and achieve their goals.
