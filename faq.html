Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and process

What is Zylox and what services do you offer?

+ −

Zylox is a professional freelancing agency founded by Moeed Mirza that specializes in web development and Shopify solutions. Our core services include:

  • Web Development - Custom websites and web applications
  • Shopify Development - Store setup, customization, and app integration
  • Custom Shopify Theme Development - Unique, brand-aligned store designs
  • E-commerce Solutions - Full-service online store development
  • UI/UX Design - User-centered design for digital products

Where is Zylox located?

+ −

Zylox is based in Chenab Orchard, Gujrat, Pakistan. However, we work with clients globally and have successfully completed projects for businesses in North America, Europe, Australia, and across Asia.

What makes Zylox different from other agencies?

+ −

What sets Zylox apart is our commitment to three core principles:

  1. Results-Driven Approach: We focus on delivering solutions that achieve tangible business outcomes, not just aesthetically pleasing designs.
  2. Technical Excellence: Our team stays at the forefront of technology trends and best practices to ensure your project is built with the most appropriate, future-proof technologies.
  3. Transparent Communication: We maintain clear, consistent communication throughout the project lifecycle, ensuring you're always informed about progress, challenges, and solutions.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance after project completion?

+ −

Yes, we offer a range of support and maintenance packages tailored to your needs. These can include:

  • Regular updates and security patches
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Content updates and management
  • Feature enhancements and additions
  • 24/7 emergency support (for premium plans)

We'll work with you to determine the right level of ongoing support for your project.

Can you redesign my existing website or Shopify store?

+ −

Absolutely! Website and store redesigns are a core part of our services. Our redesign process includes:

  1. Comprehensive audit of your current site/store
  2. Identification of performance issues and improvement opportunities
  3. User experience analysis
  4. Strategic redesign planning
  5. Implementation with minimal disruption to your business

We can also perform partial redesigns of specific sections if you don't need a complete overhaul.

What technologies and platforms do you work with?

+ −

We have expertise in a wide range of technologies:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Tailwind CSS
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js
  • Databases: MongoDB, Firebase
  • E-commerce: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento
  • CMS: WordPress, Shopify, Contentful
  • Design Tools: Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop

We select the most appropriate technology stack based on your specific project requirements.

What is your project development process?

+ −

Our development process follows these key phases:

  1. Discovery: We learn about your business, goals, and requirements through detailed consultations.
  2. Planning: We create a project roadmap with timelines, deliverables, and milestones.
  3. Design: We develop wireframes and visual designs for your approval.
  4. Development: Our team builds the solution using the appropriate technologies.
  5. Testing: We thoroughly test for functionality, performance, and compatibility.
  6. Deployment: We launch your project in a controlled manner.
  7. Review & Support: We monitor post-launch performance and provide ongoing support.

Throughout this process, we maintain regular communication and provide opportunities for feedback and revisions.

How long does a typical project take to complete?

+ −

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity:

  • Simple Shopify store setup: 2-3 weeks
  • Basic website: 3-4 weeks
  • Custom Shopify theme: 4-6 weeks
  • Complex web application: 2-4 months
  • E-commerce platform with custom features: 2-5 months

We'll provide a specific timeline estimate during the proposal stage after understanding your project requirements.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

+ −

Feedback and revisions are an integral part of our process:

  • We include specific revision rounds in each project phase (design, development, etc.)
  • We use project management tools that allow you to provide detailed feedback directly on designs and features
  • We schedule regular review meetings to discuss progress and gather feedback
  • We maintain a structured change request process for modifications outside the original scope

Our goal is to ensure the final deliverable matches your vision while maintaining project timelines.

How much do your services cost?

+ −

Our pricing is customized based on project requirements, complexity, and timeline. As a general guideline:

  • Basic websites: $1,000 - $3,000
  • Shopify store setup: $1,500 - $4,000
  • Custom Shopify theme development: $3,000 - $8,000
  • Complex web applications: $5,000 - $15,000+
  • E-commerce platforms with custom features: $6,000 - $20,000+

For an accurate quote, please contact us with your specific project details.

Do you offer payment plans?

+ −

Yes, we offer flexible payment options to accommodate different budgets and project sizes:

  • 50/50 payment split (50% upfront, 50% upon completion)
  • Milestone-based payments (aligned with project phases)
  • Monthly installment plans for larger projects

We'll work with you to find a payment structure that fits your financial needs while ensuring project progress.

Are there any hidden costs I should be aware of?

+ −

We pride ourselves on transparent pricing. Your project quote will include all development costs. However, there are some external costs to consider:

  • Domain registration ($10-20/year)
  • Web hosting ($5-50+/month depending on requirements)
  • Shopify subscription ($29-299/month)
  • Premium plugins or extensions (varies)
  • Stock photography or custom illustration (if needed)
  • Ongoing maintenance and support (if chosen)

We'll clearly identify these potential costs in our proposal so you can budget accordingly.
